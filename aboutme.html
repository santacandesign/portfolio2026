What I'm looking for in my next role

Growth

I want to have better conversations about product — work with smart people that love their jobs. Growth happens in many ways but more often accelerated growth happens in places where people feel safe to experiment, get acknowledged for their work and work with people who they like being around. There is a lot of subjectivity to who one might like being around but I find that when people feel seen, heard & understood — they are able to bring their best selves to work.

Long term commitment

I want to stick around and grow with a business, understand what it takes to be responsible for a product long term, what truly understanding your users feels like overtime when you keep releasing things that work. I feel like the satisfaction to being in close proximity with a product, seeing it stumble and succeed is irreplaceable.

Work I find interesting + Believe in

Working on a product and its features I truly believe in. Scope to take on more work and do the main work, the side work to keep the machine well oiled and running — like design systems — and getting a holistic view of the work.
