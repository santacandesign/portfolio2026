Price Insights - Is this property priced fairly?

Problem Statement

Propertyguru is a property listing site that 200k+ people use across Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia & Vietnam to find houses that they might be interested in renting/buying.

On PropertyGuru, property prices are often listed higher than their true market value, with the expectations that there will be negotiations. There isn’t a clear way to understand if a property is priced fairly, how it compares to similar properties, or how much room there is to negotiate.

PropertyGuru needed a way to **demystify pricing** by helping them quickly assess price fairness and make confident, informed decisions within their budget.



Hypothesis

**Property buyers/ renters are price sensitive** and want a good bargain for their money. Knowing the best a property has to offer and understanding how it compares to other properties helps them also negotiate better. The user’s goal is to work within their budget and get the best offer in terms of location, amenities and personal preference. **Knowing the fairness of a price is a big part of finding the right home for you.**

Why is this feature important for users?

By contextualizing a property’s listed price against similar listings and past transactions, users gain a concrete reference point instead of relying on gut feel or agent guidance alone. This helps them:

  • Understand whether a property is fairly priced, overpriced, or a good deal
  • Feel confident negotiating, backed by data rather than assumptions
  • Make faster, more informed decisions within their budget constraints



Why is this feature important for business?

**Price Insights changes PropertyGuru from property listings platform to a neutral, data-backed advisor**. This delivers business value in several ways:
  • 1. **Higher engagement & retention** Strong adoption (20% in the first month) and sustained daily usage of price history indicate that users return specifically for decision-support, not just browsing.
  • 2. **Differentiation in a crowded market** While listings can be replicated, proprietary insights derived from historical data and comparisons are hard to copy giving Propertyguru an upper hand
  • 3. **Better marketplace health** Informed users negotiate realistically, which can lead to: - Faster deal closures - Reduced friction between buyers and agents
  • 4. **Foundation for future monetization** Once users trust price insights, it opens doors to premium features for investors, agents, and serious buyers—without compromising user trust.

Research

Objective

This was a new feature that needed a thorough understanding how it would impact a user’s experience on the property listing 1. Is this a necessity for users looking to buy/rent houses? 2. What is the comprehension on the data mentioned? 3. If understood, do they find it useful? 4. Is it enough data to decipher fairness of Property prices?

Research recruitment

1. 20 participants in malaysia and singapore 2. Included both renters and buyers 3. Key spectrum were recruited from both ends of data savy-ness For ex : Investors (well versed with data savy-ness), first time home buyers, renters in malaysia (students on average don’t rent a home for more than a year or two)

Design Brief

Design a feature that allows home buyers/renters to understand fairness of a price of a property.

Process

Features of the design
  • 1. Propertyguru’s approximate estimate of property price
  • 2. Property price range of properties listed on our site with similar amenities and features

User Testing

An overall insight for this testing was that users found it extremely overwhelming and found it more confusing than when they didn’t know this information. Two of the renters in Malaysia found it completely unnecessary for renters especially

Testing round 2

On landing 1. Better copy 2. included only essential information and rest under a button, on a different page 3. Past transactions to it’s previous state which people understood very well and found it extremely important Detail page 1. Visualizations that help digest information easier and slightly more information and an option to see other listings that are similar to this including features like range and number of other listings

Outcome

1. Great comprehension 2. 10 out of 10 users found similar listing comparison useful 3. 8 out of 10 users found previous listing comparison useful 4. 10 out of 10 users felt more confident negotiating after viewing this data

Release and Reception
